Our Story

Meet Sagar, Our Founder

"Technology is not just about devices; it's about solving real problems."

Founded in 2014 by Sagar Kamble, DOT Technologie began as a small computer repair shop in Bhayandar with a vision to provide reliable and affordable tech solutions to the local community.

With over 8 years of experience in CCTV installation, security systems, and computer hardware, Sagar built DOT Technologie from the ground up, starting with basic laptop repairs and gradually expanding to offer comprehensive CCTV installation and security solutions.

What started as a one-person operation has now grown into a trusted CCTV installation and security technology hub serving hundreds of satisfied customers in Bhayandar. Our commitment to quality CCTV installation and customer satisfaction remains at the heart of everything we do.

Our Mission

To provide cutting-edge CCTV installation and security solutions with personalized service, ensuring our customers in Bhayandar can rely on their security systems for complete protection and peace of mind.
